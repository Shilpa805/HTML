Order Status

                

    The order status page is the final page of your store's checkout. The order status page lets your customers:
* Check the status of their shipment without needing to contact you directly re-order products.
* See real-time updates on the location of their shipment.
* Opt in to receive shipping updates by email(grocerykart722@gmail.com) after checking out by using a phone number.
* Subscribe to receive news and offers from GKart by email.
* Click to begin shopping in your online store again.
